What is Choose Fun?

Choose Fun is a new course code students can use to replace any a traditional Trinity Requirement. That one Natural Science credit you're stugggling to get can be replaced with any class under the CF code. These classes are some of the most fun, unique and creative courses at Duke with the hope that students with step ot of their comfort zone and have some fun.
